I’m a researcher working on methods for network analytics, network performances and architectures. My research interests include novel methods for data-driven networking, formal methods for performance evaluation and modeling of network architectures and protocols.
Interests. Computer Networks, Formal Verification, Deep Reinforcement Learning, Machine Learning
